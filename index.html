The Ever Epic

Saas-Bahu soap opera

"Ek Anecdote" wali Timeline | By Sanchit Soni

The movement from 90's to 21st Century saw a major shift in Indian TV industry. TV networks decided to scrap witty shows and replace them with generic, never ending and high yielding saas-bahu soap operas. Here is how a typical course of a saas-bahu 'Maha' drama unwinds.

Introduction of Mast Munda and Bholi Ladki

Bholi Ladki's character and background is shown. It could be anyone of these: Little girl, poor girl, little and poor girl, widow, bitchy girl, bossy girl, rich girl, sanskari girl, not so sanskari girl, hot girl, ugly girl, stammering girl, executive girl, not executive girl, ambitious girl,divorcee girl, urban girl, rural girl and just a normal next door girl.

Mast Munda's character and background is shown, as well.(Why am I not surprised) Helpless/dashing/not dashing/stupid/smart/executive/old/young/too young/iconoclastic/mental/ not mental/rapist/chauvinist/misogynist/successful/dominant or in rare cases of 'normal' boy.

Phase 1 (Episodes 1-10)

Bumping

Boy (M.M.) and Bholi Ladki (B.L.) lay eyes on each other for the first time by meeting with an accident or simply bumping into each other for no reason. After this, they despise the sight of each other because their first meet didn’t go that well.

Phase 2 (Episodes 11-20)

Sympathy

Mast Munda rescues Bholi Ladki from a tricky/perilous situation. In crappy slow motion they see each other. Love pours everywhere.

Phase 3 (Episodes 21-30)

Love

Love and Lovveeee everywhere.

Phase 4 (Episodes 31-40)

Marriage

Mast Munda and Bholi Ladki decide to get married. Meeting with two families. Families of both the sides come to an agreement for an ‘arranged marriage’.

Phase 5 (Episodes 41-50)

Enter: ‘The Bahu’

Month long ceremonies, rituals. Bholi Ladki aka newly wed bahu in the house. Munda’s mother, (BL’s SAAS, obviously) develops some differences with the Bahu. There is a power shift in the house and The Saas does not like it.

Phase 6 (Episodes 51-60)

Introduction of the ‘Parivaar’

Munda’s dad is a mute spectator with no say in house or family matters. Munda has 2-3 brothers, 2-3 sister in-laws, few chachas and their families, some old people etc.

Phase 7 (Episode 61-70)

Post marriage

Quintessential post marriage romance between Munda and Bahu. Munda is vella but still wears a suit and has a billion dollar company.

Phase 8 (Episode 71-100)

Pre War scenario

BA series of festival celebrations, sangeet ceremonies and pooja creates a rift between Saas and Bahu. Now saas has fully grown, 100% organic Hatred for The Bahu. War is coming.

Phase 9 (Episode 100-200)

Game On!

One particular event sets the house on fire. The ever-epic, never ending war saga between Saas-Bahu begins.

Phase 10 (Episode 201-210)

Saas Camp vs Bahu Camp

Entire Parivaar is divided between Saas camp and Bahu camp. Munda is now nothing more than a knot between two ends in a Tug-of-War rope.

Phase 11 (Episode 211-300)

Paarivaric Calamity

The Parivaar is being hit by a financial calamity/emotional calamity or someone dies in the house. The Parivaar has hit psychological rock bottom but they still wear those expensive clothes and live in the same mansion. Nobody has a real job btw.

Phase 12 (Episode 301-Till TRP hits all time low)

Ceasefire and truce

The Saas and the Bahu have come to a truce to tackle the situation. Peace has been restored.

Phase 13 (Episode: Till TRP hits all time low)

Post war

Munda and ladki have kids now. Everyone is at peace, business rose from the ashes but feels like they were never really poor. Munda’s dad is as useless and he used to be in the introduction episode. The Parivaar has 50 members now.

Phase 14 (Episode: Congratulations! You made this far)

Time leap

Time leap happens. Munda and ladki are older now. No physical changes whatsoever. Munda wears a glasses. Saas has two grey streaks on her hair. Munda’s dad occasionally appears and is still useless. Kids have grown up and have become teenagers now.

Phase 15 (Episode: Writer is dead now)

Return of the Bitchie Bitch

Bitchie Bitch can be anyone, like Munda’s ex girlfriend, ex wife, business partner, relative or cousin saas. Her soul purpose is to demolish peace and harmony in the house and bring pain in the family.

Phase 16 (Episode: infinity)

Saas-bahu unites

Saas-bahu unites forces to tackle Bitchie Bitch. Lot of plots, emotional drama, pointless ceremonies continue. Munda’s dad can’t move now.

Phase 17 :X

Bitchie Bitch is gone

Allied forces finally defeats Bitchie Bitch in her purpose. Teeangers become adult and gets married. One happy family has now 100+ members. Munda’s dad is a fossil now.

Phase 18 (Episode counter was shut five years ago)

Finally final finale

TV network finally gives up. Promo of the replacing soap opera is already out.
